Welcome to Pet World - Where Grooming and Adoption Come Together!

At Pet World, we believe every pet deserves to look and feel their best, which is why we offer professional grooming services tailored to your pet’s unique needs. From relaxing baths and expert cuts to nail trims and coat care, our experienced groomers ensure your pet leaves feeling fresh and happy. In addition to grooming, we're proud to partner with local shelters to provide a safe, loving environment for pet adoption. Whether you're looking to adopt a new family member or give your pet the pampering they deserve, we’re here to support you every step of the way. Visit us today for grooming services, adoption opportunities, and expert care for your pets!

Our Mission And Vision

Pet World's vision is a society living with happy and healthy animals, showing compassion towards them. Our mission is to care for animals and protect them from stress and cruelty through rescue and community education.

Our Objective

This we aim to achieve by promoting the protection of all kinds of animals, preventing cruelty to them, rescuing and relieving animals from all manner of suffering.
